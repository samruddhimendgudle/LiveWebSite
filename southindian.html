Dosa Recipe

1. Use fermented dosa batter.
2. Heat tawa and spread batter thin.
3. Add oil on edges.
4. Cook till golden brown.
5. Serve hot with chutney.

Vada Recipe

1. Soak urad dal for 4 hours.
2. Grind to thick batter.
3. Add salt and spices.
4. Shape vadas with hole.
5. Deep fry till crispy.
